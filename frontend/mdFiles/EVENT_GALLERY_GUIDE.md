## üìã Typical Workflow

### For Wedding/Event Photographers

1. **Before the Event**

   - Connect camera via WiFi or USB
   - Enable auto-sync in camera settings

2. **During the Event**

   - Photos automatically sync to Event Gallery
   - Real-time upload as you shoot
   - Create event folder (e.g., "Miller Wedding - Oct 13")

3. **After the Event**

   - Generate QR code for the event folder
   - Share QR with clients on-site or via email
   - Clients scan and download their favorites immediately

4. **Within 24 Hours**
   - Clients must download photos they want to keep
   - Photographer exports final edited versions to permanent storage
   - Files auto-delete after 24 hours

### For Portrait/Session Photographers

1. **Session Day**

   - Sync camera during or after session
   - Upload photos to session folder

2. **Immediate Review**

   - Generate QR code
   - Client scans and reviews on phone
   - Quick feedback on shots

3. **Client Selection**
   - Client downloads favorites within 24 hours
   - Photographer knows which photos to edit/finalize immediately
